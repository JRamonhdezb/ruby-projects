                         !!!HANGMAN GAME!!!

 Hello, welcome to the game of hangman. The game is quite simple, the computer
  selects a random word, which you must guess in just 8 attempts, if you fail 
  all the attempts you lose. In each attempt you have the opportunity to give
  a letter that you think belongs to the word to be guessed. If you guess 
  all the letters correctly you win. Good luck!
